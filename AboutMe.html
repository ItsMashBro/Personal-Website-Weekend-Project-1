Photography

Ever since I took photography in High School, I've been obsessed with capturing the world the way I see it with my own eyes. So that other people may experience what it's like to be me for a moment. Camera's intrigue me, the way they look and how they work is fascinating to me. In the time since those classes as a younger me, I've been practicing as a photographer for over 10 years now and I've totally surpassed what I originally wanted to accomplish. Would you like to see some of my work? Check out my portfolio on the next page to see some of my gallery.

Video Production and Editing

In my free time I really enjoy playing video games and as I've gotten older and gained a love for content creation, I decided I wanted to share that with other people. Everything I know about recording and editing, I've taught myself. I've spent thousands of hours learning how to do this stuff just so I can make videos that I'm pleased with.
